by the way

  • Milou is a display typeface, therefore it ought not be used in running texts.

    Strokes and voids have been drawn as to pull the eye to the optical recognition point of the characters. However, the effect is lost in too small a pointsize, and even produces a disturbing vibration during the long-term reading.

  • Milou is unicase—a lowercase-only typeface. Why would it be so ?

    1. It favors optical character recognition by the young reader.
    2. The lient likes it this way.
    3. So do I.
