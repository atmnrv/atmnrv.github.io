A tra­di­tion de­mands that this chair be made on a par with any cathedral. Eve­ry part of it which could not be seen ought to be just as per­fect­ly made as the parts which could be seen. Re­gard­less of how the chair is built, I can say that this vo­lume com­plies with the self­same prin­ci­ple of the cathedrals.

The pre­sent vo­lume is the con­ti­nua­tion of my ty­po­gra­phic work. An ex­pe­ri­ment in ta­king the web se­rious­ly as a book-pu­bli­shing me­dium. I wan­ted to de­li­ver a le­vel of qua­li­ty that you’d find in my prin­ted catalogue.

I have ga­th­ered a few pieces in a rea­der-orien­ted and short en­sem­ble. Lastly, for those who enjoy pictures more, I have taken care of including a Fine arts segment, which should give a broa­der in­sight into my work. One might call it a beau geste.
